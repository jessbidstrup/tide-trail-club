About Tide & Trail Club

Tide & Trail Club was created to make getting outdoors easier — and more social.

We believe movement should feel good, not intimidating. Our sessions are designed to be inclusive, beginner-friendly, and flexible, whether you’re brand new to walking trails or returning to exercise after time away.

We meet in natural spaces, move together at a comfortable pace, and always make time to connect afterwards.

This isn’t about racing. It’s about showing up.

What we do

Sunrise walk & dip

A relaxed coastal walk followed by an optional ocean dip. Start your day grounded, refreshed, and connected.

Social trail runs

Short to mid-distance trail runs with regroup points along the way. Come for the movement, stay for the chats.

Stretching sessions

Casual yoga, stretching, or mobility sessions by the sea — followed by coffee, of course.

Community events

Monthly sunset hikes, picnics, and seasonal adventure days designed to bring people together.

“No pressure. No egos. Just good people and beautiful places.”

— Shae M., regular attendee

Where we meet

Our sessions rotate across some of the most beautiful coastal spots on the Surf Coast, including:

  • Torquay Foreshore
  • Bells Beach Coastal Track
  • Point Addis Marine National Park
  • Anglesea River Trail

Exact meeting points are shared before each session so you know where to go.
